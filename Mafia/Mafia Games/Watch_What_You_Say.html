Watch What You Say

Introduction

This game of Mafia is brought to you by a Witch who is trying to kill everyone by the words they say.

Mechanics

Due to the way this game works, here are the Mechanics introduced so far and there binarry state of being on or off.
  • Mafia Visit: OFF
  • Town Visit: OFF
  • Vanilla Visit: OFF
  • Cult Visit: OFF
  • Last Will: ON
  • Private Chatter: ON

New Roles

Surgeon: Can protect a player and allows them to roleflip on death.
Voodoo Cleanser: Can target a player each phase (Day and Night) to prevent them from being killed by the Witch that phase.
Forger: Can modify one player's Last Will to be whatever they so desire. (Forgery's must adhear to the Discord text limit of 2000 characters.)
Witch: Can choose a word each phase (excluding common words like "the", "of", swears, and player names that exceeds 5 characters in length. Each player who says that word will perish during the phase after they say it. New words must be choosen each night. Cannot use the same word twice and the previous word Choosen will be revealed in phase change upon the new word being choosen.
