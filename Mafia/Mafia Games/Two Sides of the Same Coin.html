Two Sides of the Same Coin

Introduction

This game of Mafia is brought to you by "The Goddess of Death, Azrael the Grim Reaper."

Mechanics

This game of Mafia is set in an interdimentional rift where the voting machines respond to spirits of a certian nature.
Phases last twenty-three hours with a one hour cool down period.

While in the Netherworld, you can vote to add to the lynch vote or to exorcise a soul from the Netherworld.
