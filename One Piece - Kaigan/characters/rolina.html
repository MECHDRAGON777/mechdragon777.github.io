[ Personal Details ]

Personality

A quite cheerful girl who has taken a knack for the weather due to being raised in Weatheria. She seems to value her speed more so than her strength and is keen to read people quite carefully after her first reactions to the native Weatherians. She has always been one to observe first before striking when given the opportunity.

Appearance: Rolina is a Snowshoe Rabbit Mink of slender build and of mid-back level hair length dyed blue with violetish-pink ends. She wears a cloak that is made of conductive materials laced inside.

Aspiration(s): Aquire and protect all Weather-based Devil Fruit.
Likes: Destructive Weather, Cold
Dislikes: Heat
Goal:
  • Aquire every Weather-Fruit.
  • Learn more about her birthplace.

[ Backstory ]

Age 2: When Rolina was a young lass, she stumbled upon the Weatherian’s Balloon Ship and was not found til a day after Weatheria had long passed where Zou was. With little control over the direction and speed of where the Sky Island of Weatheria would travel due to the winds of the world, the Weatherians would take the young Mink in. The young Rolina would like the home she would be given in the sky, though every once in a while loaned for her normal island’s nature a tad bit which formed her refusal to abandon the speech-patterns associated with her tribe. She would still be too young to remember her actual parents from Zou.
Age 4: After living in the White Sea for a couple of years, she would soon become interested in what the Weatherians did and learned that the weather that she only experienced a little bit over her two years of living prior was due to what the Weatherians do every so often. This would also be around the point she started to understand that she had Electro in the first place due to being a Mink.
The Weatherians ended up teaching her quite a bit of the technological advancements of the Weatherians as well as other stuff that any young kid would need to know. She would end up being taught how to hold writing apparatus, but never how to read proper words. In the grand scheme of things, she ended up developing her own writing system unique to her that she could read as she got older.
Age 5: Around her sixth birthday, Rolina would find herself outside one of the buildings after the moon rose before accidently awakening her Sulong form for the first time. She would end up rampaging for a little bit before passing out in a an area that moonlight would not reach. From then after, Rolina was taught of reasons to avoid being in Moonlight for the time being.
Age 6: When Rolina hit the six year mark on her life-span, she would start to develop her Electro a bit and one of the Weatherians would then toss her a Staff to train her in self-defense for when she would eventually need to leave Weatheria when she was old enough. He would note that her strikes would be electrifying and would note that she might be interested in learning that there is a special type of fruit in the world that grants people strange and mystical abilities at the cost of their ability to swim. After the time sparing, she was capable of holding off a standard nuciase trying to pick on her if she ever had to, but would hopefully never need to.
Age 7: When Rolina turned seven, she seemed to be more hesitant in her will to attack the Weatherian that would spar with her ever so often to train her. While she trained to keep herself safe, she started to want to focus more so on what her opponent’s would start to do before reacting with her own defensive parrys.
When she was finished with as much training the Weatherians could give her in sparring, they would notice that not once that the Mink picked up that the weapon they gave her to spar with was not a typical Bo-Staff, but instead a Climatact, despite being told of it’s potential. They hoped that one day, she would finally learn how to use the Climatact with the remaining knowledge they would have been passing down over the years.
Age 10: Rolina would eventual lose the Climatact she was given when wshe was ten and as a result, she would end up needing to get a Metal Staff instead. She would not be hindered much by the differnt shape as she had yet to learn how to use the Climatact she lost in the first place.
Age 12: Rolina would have started to pick up some of her trade-mark attire when she turned 12 in key to her electro. She would have a cape fashioned out of a copper color in hopes that she could one day use her “natural gift” as she thought of it to better redirect attacks from her blind spot; however, she would never really understood that it would never work. She kept it for the most part due to thinking it would look cool. She would also start fantasizing over a few Devil Fruits based on what she heard from the Weatherians over the years. More specifically the fruits that were more weather oriented such as the Rumble Rumble fruit, Snow Snow fruit, and Gas Gas fruit being the three fruits she often fantasizes the most about. Seeing that her Electro practically is what she was told of the Rumble Rumble fruit, she typically thinks about it less and less. She often thinks that a weather based Devil Fruit would honor what she had been taught and has enjoyed topically since she was a young lass. Her Mink-blood often makes her suffer from the heat when Weatheria passes the hotter parts of the world which makes her long for the Snow Snow fruit specifically. Rolina also starts chronicling her life from this point in her free time when she has any.
Age 14: Rolina would begin being taught how to read the weather in areas around where she is which greatly delights her. The lessons also give her the bright idea of embedding a barometer into some insulated gloves to read the atmospheric pressure of the area she is in and also is taught how to read it and what it means; however, due to her lack of field-training, she was never able to understand the importance of the barometer she had almost destroyed in trying to embed it into her right glove. Rolina however would write down what the Weatherians taught her about weather reading and so on in her note book.

GALVANIC GYRE
[ ガルバニック・ジャイア, GARUBANIKKU JAIA ]
⚓ (Defensive) ⚓
Utilizing the copper Climatact as a high-speed rotor, Rolina spins the staff to create a localized electromagnetic field fueled by her Electro. By catching an incoming weapon or projectile within this ionized "pocket," she can fluidly redirect the momentum of physical attacks. The copper material acts as a grounding path, allowing her to parry strikes with minimal impact to her own frame.

PETRICHOR CYCLONE
[ ペトリコール・サイクロン, PETORIKŌRU SAIKURON ]
⚓ (Offensive/Crowd Control) ⚓
By manipulating the thermal bars within her copper Climatact, Rolina generates a localized high-pressure system that manifests as a swirling vortex of wind and heavy rain. The wind serves to "corral" opponents into the center of the storm, while the rapid downpour saturates their armor and skin. This turns the entire area into a massive conductor, allowing Rolina to discharge her Electro through the raindrops to strike every target within the gale simultaneously.
