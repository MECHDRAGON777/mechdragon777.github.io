[ Backstory ]

ᛇᛊᛏᛖᛚᛚᚨ believes she was abandoned on Drum Island as an infant, but in reality both of her parents were slaughtered by a Marine Commodore for their lineage when they took ᛇᛊᛏᛖᛚᛚᚨ to the local doctors on Drum Island while passing through. ᛇᛊᛏᛖᛚᛚᚨ's existence was not made public by them except to the doctors of Drum Island. By the time ᛇᛊᛏᛖᛚᛚᚨ would recover, she would have no memory of her family and would assume that the local orphanage was her own family due to not having anybody around that would know her besides the doctors that helped her. ᛇᛊᛏᛖᛚᛚᚨ was left with what could be a pronunciation guide from her family on how to read and write their own family's own system for secrecy where she ended up growing up to be a dual-linguistic, albeit her speech ends up on the destructive pitches when using it while younger; she does eventually learn to switch tones on a fly; but her writing as a result of secrecy has always been Runic in nature. Masao Fukuhara would as a result be seen as a Father Figure to ᛇᛊᛏᛖᛚᛚᚨ and would know the Lunarian Child as simply "Stella" from when the doctors took her to him.
Later on, when ᛇᛊᛏᛖᛚᛚᚨ was six years of age, she would often play in the snow around Drum Island while she would sing awfully. With no one around, she could practice to her heart's content without be reprimanded for how bad she was at singing like the average child would be. One day, while she was waltzing through the island, she would stumble upon a young Kokone right before a Blizzard would end up spurring about and without thinking, ᛇᛊᛏᛖᛚᛚᚨ would bring the young-lass to Masao for care. While ᛇᛊᛏᛖᛚᛚᚨ was not yet smart enough to understand he would not be able to help her that well, he was kind enough to welcome the new adoptive sister of ᛇᛊᛏᛖᛚᛚᚨ into the house with a somewhat warm embrace. While the girl was skittish upon waking up next to the flames of the fireplace, ᛇᛊᛏᛖᛚᛚᚨ would be their to welcome her which would most likely terrified the young human girl.
For the next four years, ᛇᛊᛏᛖᛚᛚᚨ would practice her singing, albeit usually while in private until Kokone witnessed her singing first hand as well as the semblance of flame breath while she sung. While finding her sister singing, she would end up getting a minor burn from ᛇᛊᛏᛖᛚᛚᚨ blindly singing thinking she was had privacy enough to not be found. Around this time, she was no where near good or great enough to be an idol, but she would have vastly improved after watching all of the traveling musicians come through Drum Island in the two years since she found Kokone. She would be reluctant to sing again after burning her sister, but would still yearn for the freedom it grants her, and would keep singing in private. After the incident, Kokone would bring up the talents of both of the two to Masao who would find a way to get the sisters to Jaya for their dreams as a way to cheer ᛇᛊᛏᛖᛚᛚᚨ on and allow both of the sisters to not only get better, but also try to make a name for themselves.
While on Jaya, their performances were of a mediocre accord that would cause them to struggle to make money for a bit. Eventually, the main town on Jaya would eventually come around to watch the sisters preform new songs every week as they grew in skill and repertoire for their talents. While still no where near a master of their crafts, the flame breath of ᛇᛊᛏᛖᛚᛚᚨ during the concerts would often be thought of to be that of a Dragon Zoan or another Devil Fruit being used as a spectacle since ᛇᛊᛏᛖᛚᛚᚨ's wings and the flame on her back were always hidden. The so-called Devil Singer of Jaya for the misconception of ᛇᛊᛏᛖᛚᛚᚨ having a Devil Fruit grew in popularity along with her masked Sister and were soon to be known around Jaya as the "Fukuhara Twins", despite not looking anything alike. Over the five years of them hosting Concerts, they barley had enough to scrape by until the Revolutionary Army happened to come through and pick the sisters up for a short period of time as "potential new recruits."
While spending two years with the Revolutionary Army, ᛇᛊᛏᛖᛚᛚᚨ learned how to wield a scythe in the most basic form to defend herself and as a result, has a custom microphone built into a retractable scythe in the cases that she would ever be attacked while on stage. The Revolutionary Army would also impart the knowledge of how bad the World Government and the Marines truly are on ᛇᛊᛏᛖᛚᛚᚨ which changes the tone of her concerts to be more so about peace and free from the World Government. She would begin to be seen as the mastermind behind her concerts and would be branded as a potential threat going forward if she preaches too much negativity on the World Government and her lineage of being a Lunarian gets out as she has effectively silently declared War on the World Government without actually declaring war on them. While with the Revolutionary Army, she would have mostly gotten by with her sister's cooking as a way to keep her pseudo-humanity in tact and well grounded in realism as she hones her strength for self-defense. She would also occasionally watch her sister's combat training while she was exhausted as well to see it was not just her trying to be strong for the other's sake.
Eventually, on ᛇᛊᛏᛖᛚᛚᚨ's effective 18th birthday, the sisters would be taken to Water 7 by the Revolutionary Army with being tasked to return as a proper recruit in the future after they see the world with their own eyes. ᛇᛊᛏᛖᛚᛚᚨ would go scouting around the island to locate where to host a concert while her sister looked for living quarters for the two. ᛇᛊᛏᛖᛚᛚᚨ would make quick use of her new understanding of her own biology to relatively quickly scout locations for concerts and would eventually meet back up with Kokone. With them locating what they need, ᛇᛊᛏᛖᛚᛚᚨ would start up a conversation with her usually tone of singing to ask Kokone if she would help set the world straight with peace. While ᛇᛊᛏᛖᛚᛚᚨ cannot perfectly control her flames, she has gotten to the point she can manifest constructs of flames using vocal signatures, but still needs the small Sea-prism Stone device from the Revolutionary Army to block her linage factor from manifesting flames while she sings.

[ Fighting Style ]

Ignition

Lunarians have the ability to emit fire from their bodies that they can control and weaponize in combat: allowing them to unleash powerful fire blasts or gatlings, clad their limbs and weapons in fire to empower their melee attacks, and even shape their flames into constructs, including very large ones. It furthermore seems that a lunarian can release their fire from their entire body in all directions, unleashing a massive explosion. At their strongest, a Lunarian's flames can be so potent that they appear to take on the consistency of magma. However, it is unknown if a lunarian's excessive use of their flames causes them any kind of drawback.
Other than that, lunarians are noted for their superhuman vitality and resilience, something that supposedly contributed to them being called "gods" before nearly going extinct. Lunarians have been shown the ability to withstand multiple extremely powerful attacks in succession without shedding a single drop of blood. More than that, lunarians either lose, after some time, or can deliberately turn off the flames burning at their back, which will cause them to temporarily lose said characteristic endurance, although this loss comes in exchange for a drastic boost in mobility, allowing members of this tribe to reach speeds faster than the untrained eye can follow.

[ Uta Uta no Mi ]

The Uta Uta no Mi is a Paramecia-type Devil Fruit that allows the user to teleport people's consciousnesses into a virtual space by singing.

[ Etymology ]

  • Uta (歌) is the Japanese word for "song".

[ Strengths and Weaknesses ]

The weakness of the ability is that Uta World can only be maintained as long as the user remains awake. It also consumes a lot of stamina to maintain the ability for long periods of time. Potential targets can also protect themselves from the user's power by blocking their hearing from the user's music. This even applies in the Virtual World, where a target can either hide in soundproof areas or contain the user in a soundproof containment-like field. They also can not manipulate the will of others, though they can alter their spirit bodies. This applies to objects as well. Other than that, the user is affected by the standard Devil Fruit weaknesses.
