Appearance

While mostly humanoid, Dryads do wield key traits, such as pointed ears and a variety of horns or antlers sprouting from their head, along with other animalistic features that varies from each individual. All of them wield sharp fangs and claws for purposes of survival. Some have extra fur, others have tails or hooves, and so on! Naturally, they are also quite noticeably taller than the average human! In terms of their young, Dryad young all are seem to not have the signature horns or animalistic features as the adults, instead having green skin and a shorter height than even the average human child.

Biology

Dryads wield the power of Natura, capable of harnessing, growing, and manipulating plant life to their will. Practiced Dryads are acclaimed to make a single seed grow into an entire forest, though this has yet to be seen. Naturally, their physical attributes are around five levels higher than the average human. This is even further enhanced upon undergoing Flosolis. The most notable thing about them in combat, asides potentially using their animalistic traits to their advantage, is their healing factor. Their healing factor allows them to regenerate, mending even most moderate wounds and warding ailments with ease. This makes them rather hard to keep down, especially when the environment is to their advantage.

Racial Advantages

Flosolis is a Dryad's natural phenomenon, occurring when exposed to sunlight or nearby plant life. This will do a couple of things, such as the Dryad's horns and claws growing some, but the most notable impact? The Dryad's physical attributes are increased by five levels, along with drastically increasing the power of their healing factor, even passively regaining stamina when under Flosolis.

Racial Disavantages

While the Dryad seems to be an immortal race, they do have a period of weakness that led to their enslavement and endangerment; Limos, a seasonal illness that most, if not all Dryads fall under once the fall leads into winter. When under Limos, the Dryad becomes five levels weaker, and is unable to access Flosolis due to their illness. This will often last from a week to months, leaving the presumably unbreakable race quite vulnerable to attack.
