[ Arashi Arashi no Mi ]

The Arashi Arashi no Mi is a Paramecia-type Devil Fruit that allows the user to control and manipulate storm clouds and the wind surrounding them.

[ Etymology ]

  • Arashi (嵐) is the Japanese word for "storm".
