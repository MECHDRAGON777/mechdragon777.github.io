Aerial Hostility 65/100 Structure 20 Creativity 8 Creative Implementation 12 Aesthetic Synergy 15 Functionality 10 While the level is not that bad, it does not give powerups as frequently as needed and when it does; it is to over compensate for when the difficulty goes haywire. (Such as with the sniffits in the final section.) The level asks a bit much for the average player to go through under normal powerup distanceing; yet, when you look at how it is here, it is kind of over the top with how little time you get to keep your powerups if you are not good at dodgeing while collecting your ice flowers. Alpine Incline (Balloons) 49/100 Structure 13 Creativity 15 Creative Implementation 3 Aesthetic Synergy 15 Functionality 3 While this level is insanely creative and I adore the effort put into this; I have a LOT of issues with this. For starters, the balloons are hard to distinguish what is inside the rubbery menace. I liked how you used the Foos to compliment the concept of wind pushing the balloons however. That is where the good things end however. The level is very janky as you often fall off of the chains on the ballons and the although lineguide in the form of wind is clever and unique, it makes it hard to tell the path. You can also get launched at unpredictable speeds which is amplified in great extint in the final section due to the amount of control I seem to lose here. Anohter concept of unfairness would be the blimb-like ballon in th evertical section. It's introduction is not well thought out and when I blindly jumped on it like anyone without knowledge of how it would react would do; it just flung me off to the bottomless pit to the left. It would have been more fair to introduce the thing with some form of safty net first.
around_the_world 64.8/100 Structure 24 Creativity 8 Creative Implementation 23 Aesthetic Synergy 10 Functionality 9.8 At first, I thought this would be a same-old-same-old 1-1 recreation, but boy was I wrong. I enjoyed playing through this level and it is a shame it was unfinished. For the content you did finish, it was nice and relaxing. I do find it odd that you put an insta-warp at the vase connecting the fourth and fifth sections instead of a pipe warp and the way you enter the pyramid. My main quarrel with this level is the difficulty balance and the edge of the screen insta-warps. (That and the fact you can swim in waterfalls.) I have seen this concept done in the past and I do say I like this implementation of it; just wish there were pipe-warps or doors instead of insta-wraps between sections. Not much to say about the vanilla aesthetic this holds.
