[ Backstory ]

Once upon a time in the world of One Piece, on the treacherous seas of the Grand Line, there were two young boys named Celios and Helios. They were twins, born into a notorious family of pirates .
Their parents, Captain Darion and Lady Johanson , were fearsome pirates who ruled the seas with their formidable crew. Their crew was notorious for their cunning tactics, unmatched combat skills, and relentless pursuit of treasure. They were known to leave their mark on every island they set foot on, striking fear into the hearts of both marines and fellow pirates alike.
Celios and Helios grew up surrounded by the thrill of adventure, tales of conquest, and the smell of the salty sea. They were raised aboard their parents' legendary pirate ship, where they learned the ways of the pirate world from a young age. Helios, the older twin, possessed a sharp intellect and a strategic mind, while Celios was known for his unrivaled strength and unwavering determination.
However, their blissful pirate life came to a sudden halt when the Marines, led by the famed Admiral, finally caught up to their parents crimes. A ferocious battle ensued, resulting in the capture of Celios and Helios' parents. The notorious pirates were sentenced to a life behind bars in Impel Down, the infamous underwater prison.
Left orphaned and alone, Celios and Helios found themselves adrift in a sea of uncertainty. Determined to carry on their parents' legacy, the young twins made a pact to seek their own path in the world of piracy, vowing to one day rescue their parents and restore the honor of their parents' pirate legacy.
With their inherited knowledge of the seas and their formidable upbringing, Celios and Helios embarked on a journey of their own. They gathered a crew of loyal individuals who were inspired by their resilience and the legendary reputation of their parents. Together, they set sail on their own ship with the goal of becoming powerful pirates in their own right and reclaiming the legacy of their parents.
