[ Backstory ]

Orphenias would be of a hybrid race mixing the blood of a multitude of races including, but not limited to: Minks, Oni, Giants, and Shandians they eventually became known as the Raijintera tribe who through genetic alterations have a slightly more durable body that can manipulate terran forces and electricity. While nowhere near as potent as his siblings his abilities allow for a more flexible combatant.
Cloned and bred to be the perfect race, he himself lacks the same distinction as his siblings; he was meant to serve the World Government that deemed his race a failure. He grows to despise them for their mistreatment and he grows separate from his siblings because of their mockery of him.
