[ Backstory ]

Velyrea was born to the Celestial Dragon Family of "Celeste" under the name of "Yesenia Celeste" on February 29th in the year of 1088. Her mother went through an experimental surgery as a punishment for leaving Marƒ´joa for a seemingly random Sky Island in the Calm Belt; however, they left so that their currently unborn can grown up in a safer environment. Unbeknownst to her parents however, the Sky Island in question that Yesenia's Parents went to happens to be borderline Impel Down territory. The island's inhabitants were known for working for the World Government on occasion and was quickly able to identify the Celestes as Celestial Dragons and as a result, were branded as "Fallens".
After being recognized by the citizens of the island, they ended up taking the soon-to-be-mother to the island's science team for medical torture and experimentation. When they found that she was pregnant with Yesenia, they turned the freakish experiments towards the Human Lineage Factor in hopes to get some sort of weapon of out of the child. Due to recent uproars of how genetic modifications granted one of the high ranking members her strength, the doctor used the chance to try to replicate her genetics to have the child to either be a weapon for the World Government or to study her for a weakness the Revolutionary Officer. Though a freak medical miracle, Yesenia was born with wings, tail feathers, and elongated bird legs with sharp talons.
Through her infancy and early childhood, the girl was supplemented to surgical torture and attempted dismemberment on multiple occasions. While her bones were hollow, they were tougher than normal and would lead her to keeping her wings; however, the scars and pain would have been amplified due to how deep the cuts where. Due to the sheer difficulty to cut through the lass's wings, Yesenia was seen as a "Demon of the Sky". With the Winds of fate at her back and her, quite filthy hair, flowing in the wind, she would mainly use her wings to glide across the oceanic voyage; mainly due to the lack of wind in the Calm Belt. Yesenia Celeste enjoyed the brief moment of freedom until she realized that starvation was quite a serious endeavor that she would have to overcome one way or another.
