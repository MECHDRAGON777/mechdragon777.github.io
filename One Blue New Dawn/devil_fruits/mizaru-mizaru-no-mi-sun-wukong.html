[ Mizaru Mizaru no mi, Model: Sun wukong ]

The Mizaru Mizaru no mi, Model: Sun wukong is a Mythical zoan-type Devil fruit that allows the user to transform into a hybrid and full version of a monkey god at will.

[ Etymology ]

  • Mizaru(見ざる) references the 3 wise monkeys.
  • Sun Wukong(孫悟空) is the Chinese name of the Monkey King.

Fighting Style

Divine Style of Sun Wukong

  1. Staff of a Thousand Transformations: Inspired by Sun Wukong's ability to change his form, this ability imbues your bo staff with shapeshifting powers. With a swift twirl, your staff can transform into various weapons or objects, adapting to different combat situations. Each transformation grants you unique abilities, such as a spear for long-range attacks or a shield for increased defense.
  2. Golden Nimbus: This ability envelops your bo staff in a radiant golden aura, mimicking Sun Wukong's famous cloud. By leaping or striking the ground with your staff, you summon the Nimbus, allowing you to soar through the air, granting you enhanced speed, agility, and the ability to traverse great distances effortlessly.
  3. Divine Monkey's Trickery: This ability reflects Sun Wukong's mischievous nature. When activated, your bo staff becomes infused with cunning energy. As you strike the ground, illusions of multiple staffs materialize, confusing your opponents and making it difficult for them to anticipate your attacks. You can also create illusions of yourself, bewildering enemies and allowing you to strike from unexpected angles.
  4. Immortal's Resilience: Drawing from Sun Wukong's immortality, this ability enhances your bo staff with divine resilience. When activated, your staff glows with an ethereal light, granting you enhanced durability and regeneration. It allows you to withstand powerful blows and quickly recover from injuries, making the user an unyielding force on the battlefield.

[ History ]

Long ago, an enigmatic group known as the Monkey Tribe inhabited a secluded island, their profound connection with monkeys fueling their desire to harness the legendary powers of Sun Wukong, the Monkey King. After years of dedicated study, they stumbled upon a hidden cache of Devil Fruits, among them the revered Mizaru Mizaru no Mi.
Recognizing the fruit’s immense potential, the Monkey Tribe guarded its powers diligently, passing down the knowledge through generations. They became masters of the Mizaru Mizaru no Mi, honing their skills and delving deep into its capabilities. The fruit became an emblem of their lineage, endowing them with extraordinary abilities reminiscent of the Monkey King himself.
United by their unwavering bond with the Mizaru Mizaru no Mi and their indomitable spirit, the Monkey Tribe repelled invaders time and again. Their legendary exploits spread far and wide, dissuading would-be conquerors with the formidable defenses and enigmatic powers wielded by those who possessed the fruit.
As time marched on, the Monkey Tribe gradually withdrew from the outside world, seeking seclusion to preserve their ancestral traditions. The Mizaru Mizaru no Mi remained protected, its secrets entrusted to a select few within the tribe. Over the centuries, knowledge of the fruit waned, transforming it into a cryptic relic whispered about in hushed tones, enticing those daring enough to seek its profound power.
In the present era of the One Piece world, the Mizaru Mizaru no Mi and the tales of the Monkey Tribe exist as legends and subjects of speculation. Pirates, marines, and adventurers embark on perilous quests, their hearts ablaze with the burning desire to uncover the truth behind the ancient Monkey Tribe and the extraordinary powers bestowed by the Mizaru Mizaru no Mi.
