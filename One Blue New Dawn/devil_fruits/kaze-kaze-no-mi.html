[ Kaze Kaze no Mi ]

The Kaze Kaze no Mi is a Logia-type Devil Fruit that allows the user to create, control, and transform into the element of "wind" at will, turning the user into a Wind Human (風人間, Kaze Ningen). The fruit is currently in circulation.

[ Etymology ]

  • "Kaze" (風) means "wind" in Japanese.
