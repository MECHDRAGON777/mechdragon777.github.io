[ Hagane Hagane no Mi ]

The Hagane Hagane no Mi is a Paramecia-type Devil fruit that allows the user to control and become metal, allowing them to make constructs of metal on the spot or armor themselves just so.

[ Etymology ]

  • Hagane() Japanese word for Steel.

Techniques

Fighting Style

Strength of Steel

  1. Quick Forge Simply put, the power of the fruit allows the user to make metal constructs at a moment’s notice. This varies from making weapons and armor in an instant, or creating general constructs to traverse terrain or other such utilities.
  2. Metal Man:Naturally, the user is a Metal Man, meaning that they are able to tank hits no normal person can. This also means that they may naturally weigh greater than they did before.
  3. Friction Smelting: A bit trickier to do, but regardless. Using friction and grinding in order to create heat, heat to melt the user’s metals and use them however is needed.
  4. Immortal's Resilience: Drawing from Sun Wukong's immortality, this ability enhances your bo staff with divine resilience. When activated, your staff glows with an ethereal light, granting you enhanced durability and regeneration. It allows you to withstand powerful blows and quickly recover from injuries, making the user an unyielding force on the battlefield.

Server Data

Property Alteration (20%): The user can now control and manipulate their fruits property to their will. From simple metal constructs to reinforcing the user's own skin, this is about the base level of the fruit. The user's body can harden to a point where it is invulnerable to most attacks, excluding fire-based attacks. The common blade will often get stuck on if not shatter against the user's hardy body. They are able to create metal constructs or augment their body depending on the situation.
Potential Awakening (60%): This stage is simply a prerequisite towards the main awakening, your character might show signs of awakening in this stage with high stamina drain. The user can become and control liquid metal, providing a much wider range of potential to how they use the metal fruit.
Paramecia Awakening (100%): The range is heightened, you do not need to touch to manipulate, in some cases special abilities are rewarded. The power of steel within their palm. Now, the potential of this fruit is limited only by the user's imagination. A portion of the almighty power of creation, the user’s constructs can be whatever they desire, made reality.
