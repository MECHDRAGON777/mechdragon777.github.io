Early life

When Chimamire was younger, her parents told her of the tragedy of her clan at the hands of the Uchiha with out the full knowledge that it was of cause from the Raikage of the time or the fuedal lords of Kumogakure. As a result of being told of this fate at such a young age, it dramaticly shaped her to dispise the clan. This event is what allowed her to unlock her Ketsuryugan through the preasure of what she had been told and how her life could have been so much better if the events of the past have never came to furision.
