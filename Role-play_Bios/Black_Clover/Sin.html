Personality: Sin is a changeling that is a bit too friendly for her own good. Often social and is easy to be friends with, despite being a changeling who you would expect to trick and steal from you.

Appearance: While in her True Form/Changeling Form, her skin becomes paler and the pupils of her eyes fade to white. Her Human form is near identical with her Changeling form besides of the obvious racial features. For being a Changeling Hybrid, she is more proud of her Changeling half and tends to not care which form she is in. Her Human appearance reflects this as the only differences is that her eyes are have color. Red and Blue heterochromia to be specific. Sin's skin is also less pale to the extent of a normal human's while in her human form. She wears a more military based long-sleeved shirt regardless of her form and dresses like the people of Tota otherwise.

Aspiration(s): To prove she can be a magic knight, despite not being human.
