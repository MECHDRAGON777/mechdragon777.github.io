When given free reign, she has a few primal instincts in part due to the surgical genetic modifications she was forced to endure when she was younger. She has a high tendency to be forgetful as a side effect of her natural lifespan being extended in the opperation.

[ Backstory ]

When Phloraphe was a young girl, she was the Princess of Ice Island and was known as Anastasia. Anastasia was not as spoiled as: her sister, the Crown Princess or her brother, the prince; she was still treated with just the same aptitude. While she was unique in her family for not expecting such gifts, she would still use what she was given. When Anastasia was merely ten years of age, she was out venturing out in the cold wasteland where she would get lost from her guarding retainer and would be kidnapped at the age of nine. She would later learn to hate the Kidnapper and the race of people he aired from with great passion.
While her family would eventually put up "Wanted Alive Only" posters up, they would be mute as Anastasia would never look like herself again after what Frithjof would soon begin to do to her. The princess would eventually have her memories programmatically erased as she would be taught new things meticulously over the year of her torturous experimentation that would shorten her aging process giving her a longer natural life, turn her into a harpy, and turn one of her eyes red. Even the fact that she was the Princess of a kingdom at all would be striped from her. The only memories she would end up keeping would be that the island she was originaly from was very cold and almost desert like in appearance.
When Phloraphe was a young human girl, she was kidnapped by Frithjof for experimentation from her home in the New World. While she has no memory of anything before the experiments done on her, she does remember being stripped of her humanity at the hands of that cat. As a result of what she endured at his paws, she has acquired a distinct dislike toward felines and Minks. While Phloraphe could only atone to the feelings of torture through her experimentation that led her to lose her humanity.
The young girl lived quite a while in isolation not knowing anything except for the horrors she was forced to witness day in and day out that she eventually wished the worst possible fate of her kidnapper. The lass lived in torment for years before she was finally rescued. When one would look at her, no one would recognize the lass that she was prior as her aging was slowed down, but not quite stopped to match her new racial appearance. As a result of the different surgeries done to her as well as what was planned to be done to her, She was never called by her birth name, but rather the long name that is befitting what she would soon be turned into. "Phloraphe D. Lyvaene Nerylis Zeanarge" is what the doc decided to name her.
While the attempt of changing her name and adding the "D" to her name was an attempt to create a being with the Will of D; that was ultimately a failure that the lass never understood. When she was finally rescued by the Marines, Phloraphe would have lost all of her memory of her life prior to being Kidnapped, including her original birth name. To the Marines found her, they would see a girl covered in feathery rags. Once found, they got her dressed in a spare Marine outfit while taking her to Marineford for questioning; however, they would soon find out that she had no knowledge what so ever except for the name of the person who did "this" to her and her own name.
While on the Marine Vessel, the Marines offered her a job if she did not have a place to go, but due to her awkward and savage appearance, they would actually offer a guard job at Impel Down instead. The lass did not know one way or the other and would actually accept the job. She would not yet know that her personality was programmatically altered as a side effect of what Frithjof did to her; Phloraphe would actually attack the walls of the ship out of instinct to learn that she has less to her humanity than what she could remember from the years of torturous experimentation.
As a deal of promise, the few Marines who were courageous enough to teach her would instruct her on how to defend herself from inmates should they get free as well as to put them back in their place. Noticing that she would only have talons for feet instead of arms, they would not think to train her wing-muscles at all and would opt to train her in kick-related attacks. She would learn of use her powerful talons like the blade of a sword instead of standard kicks while watching the Marines fail at trying to teach her.
After a week of off and on training from the Marines, Phloraphe would find the Marines to be less of a pain than the Mink that experimented on her, but would still be cautious around them. She would reveal her name that the doctors of Frithjof's lab gave her as "Phloraphe D. Lyvaene Nerylis Zeanarge" without knowing anything about the mysterious "D" in her altered name. The highest ranking Marine on the ship told her with a straight face to refrain from mentioning her full name when possible which lead herself to be known simply as "Phloraphe Zeanarge" when introducing herself.
Upon arriving at Impel Down, Phloraphe would be stationed to guard Levels 1 and 2 after being given a run down of how Impel Down has five levels and how things actually work in the prison. She was permitted time away every so often as long as she made sure prison transit she was a part of ran smoothly and no prisoners got anything over on her during her shifts. Her newly feral instincts would as a compromise have her take assault on the prisoners instead of the other way around; Phloraphe scratching anyone and everyone that would threaten her.
Phloraphe has been stationed at Impel Down for the majority of the past two years off and on ever since her introduction to the outside world from her noble abode in the New World. While she would have unknowingly enjoyed the warmer air compared to the wintery wasteland she was originally from, every so often, she would long for her homeland every once in a while without knowing anything about it. Any time she would visit Level 5 of Impel Down for any reason, the chills from the floor shock remnants of once delete memory.
