BACKSTORY

Xæra was born over seven hundred years before the current Queen of the Harpies took the throne and lived a sheltered life for the first seventy or so years of her life after her younger sister died by falling to her death at the age of four. Xæra was eight at the time and was locked in a room in Weatheria as a result til her sixteenth birthday at which her mother, Æstrid,  returned home in a gravely wounded state. When she found her mother was in such a “Wounded” state, she tried to help her mother out the best she could, but she lacked the proper medical knowledge at the time to save her. Over the last three days of her life, her mother decided to use her Devil Fruit’s ability on her daughter to give her ever-lasting youth. As a result, Xæra has the body of a sixteen year old Harpy.

        After the sacrifice of the Ageless Surgery, her Father, Æðelwine Lambdadelta has taken her from island to island every so many years where she eventually found a guy who she fell in love with at the age of twenty, but due to her body’s secret, she was taken away from there four years later leaving a new-born Harpy in the East Blue before moving again. Before long, she carried the tradition of moving to a new island every five to ten years after her father died at the age of 97. Every time she arrives at a new island, she would claim to be sixteen and looking for work. In her spare time, she found hobbies of studying weather patterns like her family did originally before becoming a family of medical professionals.

        Most of her time has been spent treating the ill on the new islands she explored every so often that she became seen as that of a plague doctor. She had little time to explore her passion for the weather and it took her decades to find a way to control the weather before eventually using her country’s research to eventually get a Clima Tact. She has used it to cause rainfall on the islands she visited more often than not and to stop rain when the islands were experiencing floods.

         Around the age of 135, Xæra stopped visiting the White Sea all together to visit the Blues, although she could never fly through the Calm Belt; as a result, she always remained flying between the East Blue and the White Sea. After being seen as a plague doctor, she started carrying a plague doctor's mask with her which she always wears during an operation. The Islands she frequented the most would include Polestar Island, Conomi Island, Organ Island, Yatsuba Island, Dawn Island, and a few others. As she got older, she used her mask to hide her identity a bit more as Harpies became more and more prevalent in the world; however she would still not stay on the same island for more than two decades at a time when in hiding.

        In the present day, She has moved to Dawn Island recently and those that last saw her in the region would have mostly forgotten about her. She has been in Foosha Village for a few weeks at this point in time; she has not been seen without her mask since she has arrived. Xæra is often seen near the medical facility in the village since her arrival during the day and can sometimes be seen at night either sleeping on the southern shore or practicing fighting with a bo-staff in the Forest.
